Frequently Asked Questions

What is an "Agent"?

An agent is a specialized AI system that continuously monitors markets, analyzes data, and generates actionable signals. Each agent focuses on a different strategy or data source.

How are signals generated?

Signals combine technical analysis (20+ indicators), sentiment analysis (Twitter/news NLP), and macro regime scoring into a consensus recommendation per asset.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What markets are covered?

US stocks, ETFs, crypto (BTC, ETH, majors), commodities (gold, oil, silver), and DeFi protocols. We're expanding to forex and futures.

Is this financial advice?

No. Finansor AI provides data-driven signals and analysis tools. All trading decisions are yours. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

How do I get alerts?

Pro and Enterprise plans support Telegram and Discord integrations. Starter users can view signals on the dashboard in real-time.
