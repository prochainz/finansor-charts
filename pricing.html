Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI agents?▼
AI agents are autonomous programs that continuously analyze market data, generate trading signals, and monitor portfolio risk. Each agent specializes in a specific analysis type — sentiment, technical, macro, etc. — and contributes to a consensus signal.
How are signals generated?▼
Signals are generated using a multi-layer consensus system: 20+ quantitative strategies are run on each asset, weighted by their walk-forward validated performance, combined with sentiment analysis, macro indicators, and regime classification. The final score determines BUY/HOLD/SELL with a confidence level.
Can I use this for real trading?▼
Finansor AI provides signals and analysis — not financial advice. The signals are research-grade and walk-forward validated, but all trading decisions are your own. We recommend paper trading first and always applying proper risk management.
How often are signals updated?▼
Free users get daily updates. Pro users receive signals every 4 hours. Enterprise clients can get real-time updates via WebSocket API.
