Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of signals does Finansor generate?▼
Finansor generates consensus signals by running 20 quantitative strategies simultaneously on each asset. Each signal includes a direction (BUY/SELL/HOLD), a score from -1 to +1, confidence percentage, and the best-performing strategy for that asset. Signals are regime-adaptive, meaning they adjust based on current market conditions.
How accurate are the signals?▼
Our backtested strategies show win rates between 49-68% with Sharpe ratios up to 1.78. However, past performance doesn't guarantee future results. We recommend using signals as one input in your decision-making process, not as standalone trading advice. The confidence score helps filter high-quality signals from noise.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your dashboard. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What assets are supported?▼
We currently track 46+ assets including major US stocks (AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, TSLA, etc.), cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, SOL, etc.), and key ETFs (SPY, QQQ). Enterprise users can request custom asset coverage.
Is there an API?▼
Yes, Pro and Enterprise plans include REST API access for programmatic signal retrieval, agent control, and portfolio data. The API supports JSON responses with WebSocket for real-time updates. Full documentation is available in your dashboard.
How does the free tier work?▼
The free tier gives you access to 5 assets with daily signals (delayed by 4 hours), a basic dashboard view, and 3 strategies. It's perfect for exploring the platform before committing. No credit card required to sign up.
